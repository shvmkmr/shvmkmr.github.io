Hi, I am Shivam

Biologist Fusing Data Science & Healthcare for Innovative Solutions

I am a computational biologist pursuing my Ph.D. at THSTI, a government institute dedicated to translational research. With a background in computer science and a deep interest in healthcare improvement, my journey in data-driven biology has been both exciting and rewarding. In addition to my academic pursuits, I am also an adept web scraper, collecting valuable datasets from various online sources, and an enthusiastic writer, having been recognized for my scientific storytelling. My unwavering dedication is driven by the belief that data science and life sciences can reshape the healthcare landscape.

Research Interest

I explore the intersection of data science and life sciences.

  • Integration of Multi-Omics Data for Personalized Medicine
  • Machine Learning Applications in Biomarker Discovery
  • Network Analysis of Protein-Protein Interactions in Disease Pathways
  • AI-Enhanced Clinical Decision Support Systems
  • Investigating Genomic and Epigenomic Signatures in Disease Progression
  • Text Mining for Knowledge Discovery in Biomedical Literature

Projects

Highlight of my notable projects

Disease Signature Generation

Utilizing machine learning, I develop disease signatures for applications in diagnosis and prognosis, pioneering the way toward personalized medicine.

Image Analysis for Genomic Markers

My work revolves around 3D MRI images and histopathological data to identify genomic markers crucial in disease understanding and treatment.

Konnect2Prot

I've created 'Konnect2Prot,' a web application that facilitates in-depth protein-protein interaction data analysis, enhancing our comprehension of molecular biology.
